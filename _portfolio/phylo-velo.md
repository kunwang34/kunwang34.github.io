[PhyloVelo](https://github.com/kunwang34/PhyloVelo) infers cell-state transition trajectories by integrating lineage tracing information with single-cell transcriptomic measurements. It improves velocity estimation by incorporating phylogenetic constraints and has been applied across development, cancer, and immune contexts.
