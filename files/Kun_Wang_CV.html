Introduction

Computational biologist with a PhD in probability theory and mathematical statistics. Specializes in stochastic dynamical modeling and statistical inference for phylogeny-resolved single-cell data. Developed computational frameworks integrating lineage tracing and transcriptomics to reconstruct developmental dynamics and infer cell fate decisions. Led the development of PhyloVelo (Nature Biotechnology, 2023) and scPhyloX (Cell Systems, 2025), with additional contributions to mitochondrial lineage tracing analysis (Genome Biology, 2025). First author of a review article accepted in Nature Reviews Genetics.

Research Contributions

PhyloVelo

June 2021 – June 2023

PhyloVelo infers cell state transitions using lineage-resolved single-cell data.

  • Published in Nature Biotechnology (2023).

  • Improves velocity estimation by incorporating lineage structure.

  • Robustly maps developmental trajectories across diverse biological systems.

scPhyloX

Oct 2020 – Mar 2025

scPhyloX infers tissue growth dynamics from single-cell lineage trees

  • Published in Cell Systems (2025).

  • Quantifies diverse stem cell growth rates across different tissues.

  • Detects Darwinian selection and expansion of advantageous tumor clones.
